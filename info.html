tutorial

you have 6 attempts to guess an enemy correctly, if you go over 6 guesses you lose. If a section is green it means its correct, if its a section is yellow it means you are close, if its red youre wrong and its not that close.

yellow color thresholds

  • Health: within 50 hp of the target.
  • Total Waves: within 6 total waves of the target.
  • Quick Note: total waves count includes attrition and hell, as while no enemies debut in either mode they do appear in it. w1-9 of mastermind wont be included, as those waves are controlled by the player.
  • First Encounter: within 2 waves of the target.
