you have 6 attempts to guess an enemy correctly, if you go over 6 guesses you lose. If a section is green it means its correct, if its a section is yellow it means you are close, if its red you're wrong and its not that close.

  • Health: within 50 hp of the target.
  • Total Waves: within 6 total waves of the target.
  • First Encounter: within 2 waves of the target.
  • Quick Note: total waves count includes attrition and hell, as while no enemies debut in attrition and only 1 enemy debuts on hell the rest do appear in them, w1-9 of mastermind wont be included, as those waves are controlled by the player, if an enemy is spawnable in sandbox through spawn units (or vehicles coz chassis) that will count as an encounter to their total waves.
